OUR STORY

Who We Are & What We Do

GlassGuard was born from a modern challenge: we spend an average of 7 hours a day staring at screens. This relentless exposure to harmful blue light leads to eye fatigue, headaches, and disrupted sleep. Our mission is to provide an elegant, physical solution without compromising your screen's clarity or color accuracy.

Designed for students, professionals, and everyday tech users, our premium tempered glass screen protectors filter out harmful wavelengths while offering top-tier physical protection for your devices.
