OUR STORY

Who We Are & What We Do

GlassGuard was born from a modern challenge: we spend an average of 7 hours a day staring at screens. This relentless exposure to harmful blue light leads to eye fatigue, headaches, and disrupted sleep. Our mission is to provide an elegant, physical solution without compromising your screen's clarity or color accuracy.

Designed for students, professionals, and everyday tech users, our premium tempered glass screen protectors filter out harmful wavelengths while offering top-tier physical protection for your devices.

HOW IT WORKS

Science meets simplicity

Our proprietary nano-coating technology embeds blue-light-filtering particles directly into the glass during the tempering process. Unlike software solutions that dim your screen, GlassGuard works at the physical level — blocking harmful wavelengths while preserving the vibrant colors you love.


  • 01 Clean — Wipe your screen with the included alcohol pad and microfiber cloth.
  • 02 Align — Use the positioning frame to place the protector with perfect accuracy.
  • 03 Apply — Press gently from the center outward — bubbles disappear automatically.
