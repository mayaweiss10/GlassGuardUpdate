HOW IT WORKS

Science meets simplicity

Our proprietary nano-coating technology embeds blue-light-filtering particles directly into the glass during the tempering process. Unlike software solutions that dim your screen, GlassGuard works at the physical level — blocking harmful wavelengths while preserving the vibrant colors you love.


  • 01 Clean — Wipe your screen with the included alcohol pad and microfiber cloth.
  • 02 Align — Use the positioning frame to place the protector with perfect accuracy.
  • 03 Apply — Press gently from the center outward — bubbles disappear automatically.
